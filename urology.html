Urology

For urologists, deep pee insights matters. IoT electronic sensors seamlessly integrated into the uroflow monitoring system provide a goldmine of precise data on renal function that help to quickly detect warning signs, so that you are able to confidently recommend the right treatment plan early. These Void sensors are capable of constant and realtime measurement and monitoring without affecting patient comfort. Simple, easy to use, they are also cost effective. Not to mention, the potential for human error in current urine flow rate assessment techniques is avoided.
