Equipment maintenance

Industry leading expertise and innovation have enabled IoT connected devices powered by Artificial Intelligence to provide quality avenues to preventive maintenance that maximize uptime of equipment and cut operational costs significantly. In a way, the embedded devices are your early warning system to the health of your key assets and ensure uninterrupted delivery of quality healthcare. By tagging hospital equipment with advanced sensors and Ai, you are able to monitor movable and immovable assets in real-time; and predict faulty anomalies before they magnify, upgrade existing equipment and easily locate misplaced high value assets.
