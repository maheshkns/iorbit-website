About ISO 13485:2016 certification

ISO 13485:2016 is a tested and proven international standard for Quality Management Systems in the medical device industry. An ISO 13485:2016 certification indicates that the certified company employs best practices and complies with the regulatory requirements. A certified company also enjoys better control over the processes and drives towards continual improvement.
