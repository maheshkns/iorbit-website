iOrbit is ISO 13485:2016 certified

iOrbit Digital Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is proud to announce being ISO 13485:2016 certified recently

Our decision to become ISO 13485:2016 certified is a proactive one that demonstrates a commitment towards quality in design and development of devices and cloud-based server software for IoT enabled products in the healthcare domain. iOrbit is committed to improving the processes and mechanisms of product development and delivery with the goal to achieve the highest levels of product and service qualities. iOrbit is committed to improving the processes and mechanisms of product development and delivery with the goal to achieve the highest levels of product and service qualities.

About ISO 13485:2016 certification

ISO 13485:2016 is a tested and proven international standard for Quality Management Systems in the medical device industry. An ISO 13485:2016 certification indicates that the certified company employs best practices and complies with the regulatory requirements. A certified company also enjoys better control over the processes and drives towards continual improvement.
