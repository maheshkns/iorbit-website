About us

Get to know us, our partners and our team

The iOrbit Story

iOrbit is a digital health company founded over 4 years ago. Our focus is to enable the convergence of clinical aspects, behavioral sciences and technology.

At the heart of what we do is the iThings Connected Medical Devices Platform (iThings CMD).

iOrbit currently has sales, marketing and development teams spread across Europe, US and India. The strategic directions for iOrbit are provided by the leadership team that comprises global experts from the domains of clinical sciences, behavioral and data sciences, global sales, funding and M&A and technology.

The product development activities that we do for our customers are organized as ‘studios’ to reflect the originality and creativity that we strive to bring to the table for any of the activities we do. We currently have five studios focused on Product Conceptualisation, User Experience Design, Hardware Design, Software Design and Cloud Design.

iOrbit also has strategic partnerships with key players in the areas of expertise including world renowned universities and research groups.

We value your feedback

We are fully aware of the fact that at the crux of what we do is the global family of physicians and clinicians who are striving every day to ensure that their patients receive the best care that is possible, of patients who aspire to enjoy a high quality of life even while they are undergoing medical diagnosis and procedures to deal with health challenges and suppliers of medical devices and technologies who want to ensure their customers get the most reliable solutions that are state of the art.

No matter which segment you belong to, we are eager to hear from you so that we can continue to improve our processes, methodologies and practices to ensure you get the best results from us.
