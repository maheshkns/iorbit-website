Audiology

In this age and time, when most hearing aids generally just amplify sound making it hard and difficult to hear in noisy environs, advanced digital processing technology paired with the intelligence ingenuity and innovation of iOrbit is providing finesse in hearing capacity. Through the use of error-free sound analysis, unwanted background noise in various area settings is filtered out and amplifies only what they want to hear ensuring optimum clarity. For the convenience of hard of hearing seniors and the deaf, data from the audiologist’s computer can be sent to their smartphone which is then relayed to the hearing aid via Bluetooth – allowing the audiologist direct access to the device from anywhere, anytime.
