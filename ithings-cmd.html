iThings CMD

Connected Medical Devices

You take care of the patient, MUSA takes care of you



iThings CMD is an IoT platform that enables real-time Remote Patient Monitoring by leveraging latest IoT devices and sensors, platforms and technologies.


iThings CMD facilitates digital patient engagement by collecting remote patient data on a real time basis through IoT devices like sensor, wearables, medical devices. The platform makes remote patient data available to the clinicians, caregivers and hospitals enabling them to take immediate remedial action.


iThings CMD implements industry standards to ensure total data security and privacy of sensitive patient data and personal information.


iThings CMD enables seamless integration with EHR systems to update patient health status collected through connected devices.

iThingsHealth for elderly care

A state-of-the-art digital technology enabled telecare system powered by iThings CMD to enable independent and affordable living to the elderly people and those with long-term care needs. iThingsHealth is powered by IoT where sensors will monitor activities in daily living and detect events such as fall, heart attack with a 24 hours response centre. Should a sensor be activated, an alert will be immediately received at the monitoring centre or caregivers to provide help.

iThings CMD platform solution for leading pelvic-health device manufacturer

Leveraging the iThings CMD platform, iOrbit enabled the current standalone product to make it compatible with a connected cloud platform, so that they work seamlessly. This solution enables easy data sharing between environments and solves the problem of manual backup by the users. iThings CMD also enabled the end-to-end deployment of the cloud connected home variant of the product which is to be used by patients at home under a specialist’s guidance.
