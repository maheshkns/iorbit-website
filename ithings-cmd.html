iThings IoMT

iThings IoMT platform that enables real-time Remote Patient Monitoring by leveraging latest IoT devices and sensors, platforms and technologies.


iThings IoMT facilitates digital patient engagement by collecting remote patient data on a real time basis through IoT devices like sensor, wearables, medical devices. The platform makes remote patient data available to the clinicians, caregivers and hospitals enabling them to take immediate remedial action.


iThings IoMT implements industry standards to ensure total data security and privacy of sensitive patient data and personal information.


iThings IoMT enables seamless integration with EHR systems to update patient health status collected through connected devices.
