Why iOrbit?

Do you believe in creating a healthier world?

We are a global company with development teams in Atlanta, Netherlands, India. Our focus is to improve digital health offerings globally bringing together the best of the three domains Clinical, Behavioral Sciences and Technology. You will join a team of people from each of these streams that are passionate about how the advances in each of these streams can be brought together seamlessly to deliver world class care to a global customer base.

Open Positions

Full Stack Developer


Skills and Expertise

  • Design overall architecture of the web application.
  • Maintain quality and ensure responsiveness of applications.
  • Collaborate with the rest of the engineering team to design and launch new features.
  • Maintain code integrity and organization.
  • Experience working with graphic designers and converting designs to visual elements.
  • Understanding and implementation of security and data protection.
  • Highly experienced with back-end programming languages {{Ex: PHP, Python, Ruby, Java, .NET, JavaScript etc}}
  • Proficient experience using {{Ex: advanced JavaScript libraries and frameworks such as AngularJS, KnockoutJS, BackboneJS, ReactJS, DurandalJS etc.}}.
  • Development experience for both mobile and desktop.
  • Understanding of server-side languages including {{such-as Jade, EJS, Jinja, etc.}}.
  • Experience with cloud message APIs and usage of push notifications.
  • Knowledge of code versioning tools {{such as Git, Mercurial or SVN}}.

Profile

Required skills:

  • At least 5 years of professional experience in mechatronic and motion control software development
  • Bachelor/Master degree or equivalent in Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or software programming for mechatronic systems
  • High proficiency in C, MATLAB, Simulink, Real Time Operating Systems (RTOS), DevOPs
  • Experience with shell scripting, Python, Linux, makefiles
  • Familiarity with code generation and compilation MATLAB, Simulink using embedded coder and others..
  • Deep understanding of how an embedded system kernel is created, loaded and run.
  • Experience with embedded protocols (EtherCAT master stacks)
  • Experience with version control systems (Subversion) and development processes (Agile, Scrum)
  • You have strong Linux system administration skills.
  • Full time availability and flexible attitude needed for startup environment
  • Open, team-player attitude and excellent communication skills
  • Proficiency in English (written and oral)

Offer

We are offering a full time challenging and engaging position for the right candidates who aspire to make a meaningful difference in health care domain

Interested? If you are interested , please apply with your CV and a short letter indicating why you are passionate about this job profile to hr@iorbit-tech.com
