Why iOrbit?

Open Positions

Robotics SW Engineer


Job description/background

The GEN2 system designed by Microsure is rolled out for use in a number of selected medical centers. Feedback from these centers will be used to provide input for new design iterations. We are currently looking for a mechatronics software developer who is able to define and implement a new RTOS software building pipeline and additional develop and test SW design features and functionalities for our micro surgical robotic systems.

Tasks and responsibilities

Within a multidisciplinary team of developers you will be responsible for designing and implementing motion control SW and a new DevOps architecture using Matlab, Simulink, Embedded Coder, RTOS, Linux, etc. with main focus on safety, reliability and performance:

  • Based on specified requirements, design and realize new features and functionalities for current and future device configurations
  • Design, implementation and verification of embedded SW and operational SW, running simulations, executing (unit) tests, creating test reports, log files
  • Integration of SW (sub)systems into system level, executing system level verification, creating test reports
  • Bug finding and fixing, regression testing
  • Work according to relevant medical device norms/standards (IEC 62304 and IEC 60601-1 and 60601-1-2)

Profile

Required skills:

  • At least 5 years of professional experience in mechatronic and motion control software development
  • Bachelor/Master degree or equivalent in Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or software programming for mechatronic systems
  • High proficiency in C, MATLAB, Simulink, Real Time Operating Systems (RTOS), DevOPs
  • Experience with shell scripting, Python, Linux, makefiles
  • Familiarity with code generation and compilation MATLAB, Simulink using embedded coder and others..
  • Deep understanding of how an embedded system kernel is created, loaded and run.
  • Experience with embedded protocols (EtherCAT master stacks)
  • Experience with version control systems (Subversion) and development processes (Agile, Scrum)
  • You have strong Linux system administration skills.
  • Full time availability and flexible attitude needed for startup environment
  • Open, team-player attitude and excellent communication skills
  • Proficiency in English (written and oral)

Desirable skills:

  • Experience with communication protocols (TCP/IP, EtherCAT)
  • Experience in a regulated industry such as Medical, Automotive or Aviation
  • Hands-on experience and ability to assemble/disassemble mechatronic systems, for development purposes

Offer

We are offering a full time challenging and rewarding job in the fast growing field of medical robotics. Depending on your capabilities and ambitions you can grow within Microsure.

Interested? If you are interested, please apply with your CV and a short letter of motivation to Lars Schiemanck, l. careers@iorbit.com
